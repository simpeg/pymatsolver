Solver.solve(rhs)[source]#

Solves the system of equations for the given right hand side.

Parameters:
rhs(…, M, N) or (M, ) array_like

The right handside of A @ x = b.

Returns:
x(…, M, N) or (M, ) array_like

The solution to the system of equations.
